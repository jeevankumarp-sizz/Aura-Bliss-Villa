Welcome to
Aura Bliss Villa

Tucked away in the peaceful village of Edayanchavadi, just minutes from Auroville's spiritual heart, Aura Bliss Villa offers a rare blend of privacy, comfort, and natural beauty. This isn't just a stay — it's a retreat into calm.

Wake to birdsong, spend lazy afternoons by your private pool, and return from the beach to a home that feels entirely your own. Every detail — from the spacious bedrooms to the fully equipped kitchen — is designed for effortless relaxation.
